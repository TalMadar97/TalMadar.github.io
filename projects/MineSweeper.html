Minesweeper

Welcome to Minesweeper! Try to clear the grid without clicking on a mine.

How to Play:

  • Click on a cell to reveal what's underneath.
  • If it's a number, it shows how many mines are adjacent to that cell.
  • If it's a blank space, it means no adjacent mines, and surrounding cells will be revealed.
  • If you click on a mine, the game is over.
  • You can flag cells where you think there might be a mine by right-clicking.
